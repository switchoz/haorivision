## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Caption Timing

- NARRATOR captions: 5-8 seconds display
- ARTIST captions: 8-12 seconds display
- PAUSE: No caption, just wait

### Caption Types

- `narrator` — Green border, system font
- `artist` — Pink border, artistic font
- `system` — Blue border, technical messages

### Live Mode

Operator can trigger captions manually from `/admin/show` panel or they auto-trigger based on timeline cues.
