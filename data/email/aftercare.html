🧼Washing & Cleaning

Hand Washing (Recommended)

  • Fill basin with cold water (max 30°C / 86°F)
  • Add mild silk detergent (pH neutral)
  • Gently submerge haori and swirl for 2-3 minutes
  • Rinse thoroughly with cold water until soap-free
  • Never wring or twist the fabric

⚠ Never Do This

  • Machine wash (damages UV paint and silk fibers)
  • Use bleach or harsh chemicals (destroys fluorescent pigments)
  • Hot water (shrinks silk and fades colors)
  • Tumble dry (ruins structure and glow effect)

💧Drying

Air Dry Only

  • Lay flat on clean towel, reshape gently
  • Avoid direct sunlight (fades UV paint over time)
  • Dry in well-ventilated area
  • Allow 24-48 hours for complete drying
  • Never hang while wet (stretches shoulders)

💡 Pro Tip

Roll the haori in a clean towel to absorb excess water before laying flat. This speeds up drying without damaging the fabric.

👔Storage

Proper Storage

  • Hang on wide padded hanger (prevents shoulder marks)
  • Store in breathable garment bag (not plastic)
  • Keep in cool, dark, dry place
  • Avoid compression or folding (creases UV paint)
  • Use cedar or lavender sachets to prevent moths

✨UV Glow Care

Maintaining Fluorescence

  • Minimize sun exposure (UV light slowly fades fluorescent pigments)
  • Test UV lamp regularly (included lamp lasts 10,000+ hours)
  • Clean glow areas gently (no rubbing or scrubbing)
  • Recharge glow occasionally under blacklight (10-15 minutes)
  • Expected lifespan: 5-10 years with proper care

💡 Glow Restoration

If glow seems dimmer, expose to included UV lamp for 10-15 minutes. This "recharges" the fluorescent pigments. If glow has significantly faded after 3+ years, contact us for professional restoration service.

🛡️Repairs & Touch-ups

Professional Services

  • Minor repairs: Small tears or loose threads — contact us within warranty period
  • UV restoration: Faded glow areas can be repainted (fee applies after warranty)
  • Cleaning service: Professional art textile cleaning available
  • Companion painting care: Same guidelines apply to framed canvas

⚠ Warranty Voidance

  • Bleached or chemically treated
  • Modified, altered, or DIY-repaired
  • Exposed to extreme heat or open flame
